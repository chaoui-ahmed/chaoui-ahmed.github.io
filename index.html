RESEARCH PROJECT · TIPE
The Physics of Surfing
Wave Physics

A two-year independent research project (TIPE) on the conditions required to surf a breaking wave. Modelled shallow-water breaking waves with Airy linear theory (and Stokes higher-order corrections), studied swell propagation and shoaling, then quantified the lift, drag and buoyancy forces on a balsa board and the surfer's balance across pitch, roll and yaw. It led to admission to EURECOM through the Concours Mines-Télécom.
